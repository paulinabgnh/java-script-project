Game Instructions:

The game board: The board consists of rows and columns. Different shapes fall from above all the time.
The shapes: The shapes come in a variety of different geometric shapes, such as squares, rectangles, triangles, etc.
Move and rotate: You can move the falling shape to the side and rotate it, to find its ideal position on the board.
rows: When a full row is created, it disappears from the board and all rows above it are moved down.
Lose: The game ends when the shapes reach the top edge of the board and you fail to form rows
